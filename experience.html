North Carolina Governor's School West

Instrumental Music | 2017

The North Carolina Governor's School is a four-week summer residential program for gifted and talented high school students, integrating academic disciplines, the arts, and unique courses on each of two campuses. Learning focuses on the exploration of the most recent ideas and concepts in each discipline, and it does not involve credit, tests, or grades. Students are selected to attend through a competitive process after being nominated by their public school unit or non-public school.

NCGSW is the most impactful programs I've ever experienced. While attending, I was challenged to think critically in a ways that I never encountered previously. NCGSW put emphasis on contemporary ideas, topics, theories and techniques in both my primary discipline (instrumental music) as well as sociopolitical issues. NCGSW prepared me to respectfully discuss differing opinons and viewpoints of others. To say Governor's School was life changing is an understatement, it was absolutely incredible to be immersed in an environment that focuses on learning for the sake of learning, not grades or competitions. I am beyond grateful for the opportunity to participate in this program, it taught me the importance of fostering a community that accepts all people regardless of who they are.
